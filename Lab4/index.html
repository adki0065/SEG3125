About us

Situated in Ottawa, Ontario, we are a locally owned and operated Animal Hospital accredited by the College of Veterinarians of Ontario, the licensing body for animal hospitals and veterinarians in Ontario. our animal hospital has a long tradition of building lasting relationships with you and your pets. We are knowledgeable, caring and always respectful. Our experienced vets will explain your options in a thorough manner and will never perform unnecessary tests or procedures Services provided by our Animal Hospital range from Preventive Care to Examinations & Vaccinations; Pet Boarding and Pet Grooming; Dentistry; Spays and Neuters using laser Surgery and House Calls.

Spay/Neuter

Pets can be spayed or neutered once they reach 6 months of age. Recommended for all cats, dogs and rabbits. Spaying a pet entails surgical removal of the female reproductive tract, whereas neutering a male entails surgical removal of the testicles. Spaying a female pet avoids the inconvenience of heat cycles, eliminates life-threatening infections of the uterus, and decreases the risk of breast cancer. Neutering a male pet decreases the incidence of tumors of the anus, rectum, testicles, & prostate. Surgery may also decrease the tendency to stray or roam. Neutering a domestic rabbit can also decrease aggressive tendencies, biting, and thumping the hind feet.

X-Rays

Ultrasound imaging uses high-frequency sound waves to generate images of a pet's internal organ(s). Ultrasonography has the added benefit that it does not use radiation and images are captured in real-time, meaning they can illustrate the functioning of internal organs and blood flow. Ultrasonography is non-invasive and provides us with an additional tool in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.
